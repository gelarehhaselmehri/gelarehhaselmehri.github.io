## In short

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in Information Security at [Eindhoven University of Technology](https://www.tue.nl). I work under the supervision of [Dr. Nicola Zannone](https://research.tue.nl/en/persons/nicola-zannone) on advancing data protection, with a focus on access control for complex environments.
My contributions have been recognized by leading venues in the field, including ACM SACMAT and the Microsoft Bounty Program.

## Research areas

My research focuses on making access control systems more secure, understandable, and user-centric. I tackle multiple interconnected challenges in modern security:

Data protection in collaborative environments where multiple stakeholders need to share resources securely;

Authentication anomaly detection to prevent unauthorized access and privilege misuse; and

Explainability and transparency so that access control decisions can be understood by all parties involved.
